Their Szechuan Hot Chicken Sammy is probably their most liked item. It hits the craving for chicken and still has a nice kick to the flavor. I think after going so many times you can't go wrong with the Hot Chicken Sammy. 

As for the AtCha burger, I think there's better options. The burger itself is basic. Not in the, flavorless sense, but there's just something that it's missing for it to be a good burger for my tastes. 

Hot Dogs. They're great! Perfect for a good snack if you've already eaten. The flavors are definitely there if you're looking for something with taste. From surprising flavor (Teriyaki Mayo) to classic (Curry Cheese) hot dogs, I think the 3 there give a sort of depth of flavor that normal american dog dogs don't offer (obviously because of just ketchup and mustard).

For their drinks, in a vacuum, they aren't bad. I will say however that I'm not too picky when it comes to drinks. I'm not one judge drinks because I don't really have a sweet tooth. When it comes to tea however, there are better choices in the same shopping center especially since Molly Tea has opened up right next to it. For adults, I'd say that there are better choices. For kids however, I think AtCha has fitting flavors for kids as I hear that tea is not exactly the best for kids, in a similar fashion to how coffee is not the best for kids.
