**Location:** T4   
**Position:** Right Side  
**Notes:**  
  
1. Run in straight to the ball, not curved like they do in outside.
2. When they hit back row, you pull back and play lanes, not stand for a line shot.
3. If they're hitting RS, pull back a little more because the corner's have more space for them to shoot. 
4. Don't charge (Again) 
5. Don't go before the set.
