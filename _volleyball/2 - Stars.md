**Location:** Stars 
**Position:** Coaching?
**Notes:**  
  
Today I went to a practice for HCC through a friend. He had invited me to coach his team because they had a game coming up. I wanted to see how good they were because I'm always down to learn how to coach new people. As a volleyball player myself I am usually interested in seeing if I know my own volleyball skills. These were the things that I saw

1. They were quite new. They understood the differences between passing, but definitely lacked the skill to pass well. It takes more reps.

2. Their serves were also simple to get, not very challenging to pickup. They didn't really know how to aim. 

3. When they started scrimming, their 5-1 needs a lot of work, they have to communicate. Many times people were standing in places they shouldn't be.

All in all, if they're teaching, I think this might work out. Because afterwards, the coach offered me a position to coach them. This might be my first job out of college, which will be interesting. I guess we'll have to see. I'm looking foward to it.

Things I think would be good to work on in the future.
1. Correct 5-1 Placement for both serve-receive.
2. Communicating who's getting the ball
3. Distinguish who is playing what role.
4. Work on skills individually, Outsides on hitting, middles with blocking and pulling back .
5. General Blocking technique, General hitting technique, shifting into position (This includes in 5-1 rotating to the next from base to offense)
